Chapter One

The Discovery

It was supposed to be a normal summer.

Ten-year-old Ben Tennyson was stuck on a cross-country road trip with his cousin Gwen and their Grandpa Max in their beat-up RV, the Rustbucket.

Then a star fell from the sky.

Inside the crater, a strange watch-like device latched onto Ben's wrist — and refused to come off.

The Omnitrix — a device holding the DNA of a million alien species — had chosen its bearer.

Chapter Three

Grandpa Max's Secret

Ben always thought Grandpa Max was just... a grandpa.

Sure, he cooked weird food. Sure, the Rustbucket had suspicious modifications. Sure, he knew an awful lot about aliens.

But the truth was bigger than Ben imagined.

Max Tennyson was a Plumber — a member of an intergalactic peacekeeping organization that had been protecting Earth from alien threats for decades.

He had traveled the stars. Fought wars across galaxies. And he had retired... until the Omnitrix chose his grandson.

Now, with enemies old and new emerging, Max came out of retirement to guide Ben through a universe far more dangerous than any 10-year-old could imagine.

Chapter Five

Ghostfreak's Escape

Something was wrong with the Ghostfreak transformation.

Every time Ben used it, he felt a presence — a consciousness that wasn't his own, pushing against the boundaries of the Omnitrix.

The entity's name was Zs'Skayr — an Ectonurite high lord whose DNA had been trapped inside the watch. Unlike the other aliens, Zs'Skayr was aware.

And one day, he broke free.

Tearing himself out of the Omnitrix, Zs'Skayr became an independent villain — a ghost that could possess bodies, turn invisible, and spread darkness wherever he went.

For Ben, it was personal. This creature had been inside him. It knew his fears, his weaknesses. And now, it wanted revenge.

Chapter Six

The Final Battle

Everything had been building to this.

Vilgax — the intergalactic conqueror who had been hunting the Omnitrix from the very beginning — finally arrived on Earth at full power.

He came with an army. An armada of drones. Technology that could level cities.

But Ben wasn't the same scared kid who had stumbled onto the Omnitrix at the start of summer.

He had mastered his transformations. Learned from Grandpa Max. Found strength he never knew he had.

And in the final clash, it wasn't just one alien that defeated Vilgax — it was all of them. Ben cycled through every form, using each alien's strengths in perfect sequence.

The Omnitrix didn't just choose randomly. It chose Ben Tennyson.
